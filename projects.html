Projects & Research

A selection of research projects, tools, and explorations across computational neuroscience, brain-computer interfaces, and bioinformatics.

Current Research

Grewe Lab · INI, UZH & ETH Zurich

Semantic Analysis of World Models in Continual Learning

Investigating how world model representations and generated trajectories evolve across sequential reinforcement learning tasks. Training agents on a sequence of MiniGrid environments and semantically analyzing imagined trajectories from frozen model checkpoints to quantify retention and overwriting of prior task knowledge.

Reinforcement Learning World Models Continual Learning PyTorch
Gage Lab · Salk Institute

Quantifying LINE-1 Expression in Alzheimer's Disease

Developed a simulation framework to generate ground-truth RNA-seq datasets and benchmark transposable element quantification tools. Demonstrated that existing methods systematically overestimate TE expression; designed a modified reference transcriptome to correct for this inflation. Benchmarked across long-read (ONT, PacBio IsoSeq) and short-read platforms.

Bioinformatics RNA-Seq Alzheimer's Genomics

Knight Lab · UC San Diego

The Human Gut Microbiome as it Compares to Animals

Developed a QIIME2 plugin performing nested classification to identify the most phylogenetically similar animal gut microbiome to a given human sample. Built and trained scikit-learn classifiers at each node of a custom NCBI taxonomic tree.
